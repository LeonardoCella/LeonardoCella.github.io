About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher @ Italian Institute of Technology where I have the great privilege to work with Massimiliano Pontil. Before I've been lucky to spend 3 great (and hard) years as a PhD Student @ University of Milan advised by Nicoló Cesa-Bianchi.

I recently started a research project which studies the well-known model selection problem as a non-stationary rested stochastic bandit. This research benefits from the collaboration of Claudio Gentile and Massimiliano Pontil.

During summer 2018 I was selected for a four months internship at Amazon Berlin where I had the opportunity to work as Applied Scientist in the Music ML Team mentored by Giovanni Zappella.

Research Interests

I am interested in the theoretical analysis of online machine learning setting, specifically in multi-armed bandits. Recently I focused on nice problems involving (rested) non-stationary bandits.
