About me

I am a third year Ph.D student in Computer Science at University of Milan. I am advised by Nicoló Cesa-Bianchi.

I recently started a research project which studies the combination of transfer learning and stochastic linear bandits. This research benefits from the collaboration of Massimiliano Pontil and Alessandro Lazaric.

During summer 2018 I was selected for a four months internship at Amazon Berlin where I had the opportunity to work as Applied Scientist in the Music ML Team mentored by Giovanni Zappella.

Research Interests

My research mainly concentrates on the application of Machine Learning to Online Recommender Systems, with the objective of producing efficient agents capable of learning through user interactions. Inspired by this motivation I enjoy studying novel learning settings and how to speedup the learning process.
Recently I started extending my research to online convex optimization.
