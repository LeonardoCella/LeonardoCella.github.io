About me

I am a third year Ph.D student in Computer Science at University of Milan. I (have the privilege to be) am advised by Nicoló Cesa-Bianchi.

I recently started a research project which studies the combination of multi-task learning and stochastic linear bandits. This research benefits from the collaboration of Massimiliano Pontil.

During summer 2018 I was selected for a four months internship at Amazon Berlin where I had the opportunity to work as Applied Scientist in the Music ML Team mentored by Giovanni Zappella.

Research Interests

My research concentrates on the combination of Machine Learning and Recommender Systems to produce efficient agents capable of learning from users interactions. I am particularly interested in finding novel settings of stochastic multi armed bandits starting from real use cases.
I am starting extending my research to online convex optimization and reinforcement learning.
